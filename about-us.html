About Us

Who We Are

We are an organization of change-makers who believe that every community can create a better and progressive future environment for themselves. A future not tired only to the central governments of each African country.

We started extending development to less privileged communities who were eager to develop for a better life. This moment intensified when we found that there were many such underprivileged communities who needed help with Clinics, Water and education. The Organization intend to expand and spread out in all regions of each African countries to make sure we cover all possible areas and help as many communities as possible. We want to build a world where no community feels alone and gets every possible help they need as soon as possible.

We intend to see all resources whether governments or non-governmental are not wasted through duplications.

Our Team

Meet our team of devoted project managers, IT specialists, organizational system managers, volunteers, and interns. The Afrucot staff is deeply committed to enhancing the lives of those who are less fortunate. We are what we believe in; we are our basic beliefs.

Our Core Values

Accountability

We hold ourselves accountable for our actions, decisions, and their repercussions. We make sure that everything we do is transparent and reliable.

Transparency

Transparency is the cornerstone of our operations. Through honesty and integrity, we establish solid partnerships and freely exchange information.

Integrity

We adhere to moral and ethical principles, acting with fairness, honesty, and responsibility—even when no one is watching.

Excellence

We strive for excellence by setting high standards and consistently refining our procedures to exceed expectations.
