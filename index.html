Vision

A vibrant systematic Africa where rural communities thrive, united in prosperity and dignity. All fifty-four countries or if other countries evolve in the future in Africa would be covered even in the next two hundred or so years. Irrespective of which governing style may be in place in this unpredictable world.

Mission

Empowering African villages for sustainable prosperity and well-being. Protect citizens from travelling abroad and so do die on the deserts, roads, high seas for greener pastures with less than minimum wages. I.E. Without the proper migration routes, avenues or opportunities.

Our Services

Our organization provides comprehensive community development support to underprivileged and underserved communities across Africa. We work closely with local leaders to identify critical needs and deliver sustainable solutions that empower people to shape their own future. Through this approach, we help communities build resilience, improve living standards, and foster long-term progress driven from within. A major part of our work focuses on essential infrastructure such as healthcare facilities, clean water systems, and educational environments. We help establish clinics, develop reliable water sources, and support the construction and enhancement of schools to ensure that every community has access to quality health services, safe water, and meaningful education. By addressing these fundamental needs, we create a stronger foundation for improved well-being and lasting development. We also coordinate resources among governments, NGOs, and development partners to prevent duplication and ensure that support reaches the right communities at the right time. Our goal is to expand our reach to all regions of African countries, ensuring that no community is left behind. Through rapid response efforts and capacity-building programs, we strengthen communities to take charge of their growth while ensuring that development resources are used efficiently and effectively.

Safeguarding Statement

The AFRUCOT acknowledges the duty of care to safeguard, protect and promote the environment, welfare of children, young people and women in communities, and is committed to ensuring safeguarding practice that reflects statutory responsibilities, government guidance and complies with best practice of Non-Profit Organization Secretariat of the Department of Social Welfare and the Ministry of Gender, Children and Social Protection’s requirements.

OUR PROJECTS

COMMUNITY HEALTH SCREENING CENTRE

As part of our efforts with a snail pace rate have started our operations in a community with a health screening Centre is being set up at Afrangwa, near Saltpond in the Central Region of Ghana. This is an initiative to cater for the health needs of the community. The Health Screening Centre will eventually be upgraded gradually to become a fully functional urban clinic or hospital.

COMMUNITY FARMING

The organization has been given for temporal use a parcel of land approximately 16.28 Acre with all rights to use for farming activities to support operations by the founding pioneer of the foundation. Charity begins at home.Also, we are in negotiations to acquire for use an additional parcel of land of approximately 50. acres at Breman Anhwiam in Asin District for other farming activities, also by a relative of another pioneering founder.

SPORTS

AFRUCOT through MAXGABB FOUNDATION is also organizing a community football league for the youth of Dansoman-Glefe of the Ablekuma, West Municipality in the Greater Accra Region. The Sports initiative to develop more talents in the area of sports among the youth. (In future all activities with NGO#s and governmental/ private institutions must be captured).

Computer Skills Centre

AFRUCOT is working on establishing an IT skills Centre to train more youth to acquire IT skills. The IT skills Centre will help to build the capacity of the youth so as to help them gain meaningful employment to give them livelihood.

Women Empowerment

AFRUCOT is working closely with the women organization of Ghana town community to identify women entrepreneurs to support them financially to expand their enterprise to create more job for the women in the community.

Community Dumpsite

As part of AFRUCOT initiatives to promote healthy community a community dumpsite is being constructed in Ghana town community for a sustainable waste management in the community.
